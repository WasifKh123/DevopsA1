About Me

Hi there! I am a software engineer with a Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, specializing in both front-end and back-end development. With experience in developing microservices, I am equipped with the knowledge and skills to develop efficient, scalable, and secure applications.

Throughout my career, I have honed my problem-solving and critical thinking skills and have developed an eye for detail. I am committed to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in software development, ensuring that I am always providing innovative solutions that meet the needs of my clients.

In addition to my technical expertise, I am also a problem solver and product designer with strong critical thinking skills. I enjoy taking a hands-on approach to identify and resolve complex issues and developing effective solutions that meet the needs of end-users. Whether it's designing intuitive user interfaces or optimizing performance, I approach every project with a focus on creating high-quality products that meet the needs of my clients.

I am passionate about leveraging technology to make a positive impact on society and look forward to contributing my skills and knowledge to innovative and challenging projects.
