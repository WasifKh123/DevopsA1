Certified Software Development Professional (CSDP)

The CSDP certification is offered by the IEEE Computer Society and is designedd for software engineers with at least four years of professional experience. This certification covers a wide range of topics including software design, testing, maintenance, and documentation. To obtain the CSDP certification, candidates must pass a rigorous exam and demonstrate their practical experience in software engineering.



AWS Certified DevOps Engineer - Professional

The AWS Certified DevOps Engineer - Professional certification is designed for software engineers who use the Amazon Web Services (AWS) platform to manage and deploy applications. This certification covers topics such as continuous integration and delivery, infrastructure as code, monitoring and logging, and security and compliance. To obtain this certification, candidates must have a minimum of two years of experience working with AWS and pass a rigorous exam.
